About Me

Hi! I'm Pranav, a data professional passionate about turning data into
meaningful insights that help businesses grow and make smarter decisions.

Before pursuing my M.S. in Analytics at the University of Minnesota (graduating May 2026), I spent about 2.5 years at TCS working on the Pandora project. There, I had the opportunity to collaborate with international clients and cross-functional teams, focusing on data integration and analysis — and that’s where my love for data truly took off.

Through my master’s, I’ve been building a deeper foundation in data science, machine learning,
data visualization, and MLOps, applying those skills to hands-on projects — from analyzing complex datasets
to working with natural language processing models.

I’m excited to bring together my industry experience and academic learning
to help organizations uncover insights and create real business value.
I’m open to relocation and currently looking for Spring 2026 internships and full-time opportunities after graduation.
